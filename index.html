Introduction

Welcome to HealthMate UZ. We are committed to protecting your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

By using HealthMate UZ, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use HealthMate UZ:

  • Account information: Full name, username, email address, phone number
  • Health data: Age, height, weight, BMI, disease/condition type
  • Medicine data: Medicine names, dosages, schedules, intake logs
  • Lab analysis input: Text you enter for AI-powered lab result analysis
  • Order information: Medicine orders, delivery address
  • Usage data: App activity, crash reports (anonymous)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide medicine reminder and tracking services
  • To calculate and display your BMI and health statistics
  • To process AI-powered lab analysis through Anthropic's Claude API
  • To process pharmacy orders
  • To authenticate your account securely via Firebase Authentication
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
Important: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

HealthMate UZ uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, cloud database (Firestore), and push notifications. Data is stored on Google's secure servers. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API: Used exclusively to analyze lab result text you submit. Your input is sent to Anthropic's API and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not store raw API inputs permanently.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore with strict security rules. Only you can access your own data — other users cannot view your information.

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Firebase Authentication handles password security.

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your credentials private.

Health Data Disclaimer

HealthMate UZ is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. The AI lab analysis feature provides general information only and should not replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional. Always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Medicine logs and analysis history are stored until you choose to delete them or close your account.

Children's Privacy

HealthMate UZ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
